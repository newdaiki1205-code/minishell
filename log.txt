Enclosing characters in single-quotes ( '' ) shall preserve the literal value of each character within the single-quotes. 
A single-quote cannot occur within single-quotes.

bash-3.2$ echo 'It'\''s rain'          //no need to implement back-slash
It's rain
bash-3.2$ echo "He said, \"Hello.\""   //no need to implement back-slash
He said, "Hello."
bash-3.2$  echo "He said, "Hello.""
He said, Hello.
