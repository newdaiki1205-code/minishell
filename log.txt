/Users/shiraishidaisei

E

//Enclosing characters in single-quotes ( '' ) shall preserve the literal value of each character within the single-quotes. A single-quote cannot occur within single-quotes.

"${HOME}"
bash-3.2$ echo '${HOME}'
${HOME}


//Enclosing characters in double-quotes ( "" ) shall preserve the literal value of all characters within the double-quotes
bash-3.2$ echo "'${HOME}'"
'/Users/shiraishidaisei'

//The <dollar-sign> shall retain its special meaning introducing parameter expansion
bash-3.2$ echo "${HOME}"
/Users/shiraishidaisei

If a parameter expansion occurs inside double-quotes: Field splitting shall not be performed on the results of the expansion.
bash-3.2$ var="apple     orange"
bash-3.2$ echo $var
apple orange
bash-3.2$ echo "$var"
apple     orange
